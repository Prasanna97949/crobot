Ariyan

Ariyan is an advanced mobile robotics platform designed by Crobot Technologies for education, training, and consultancy applications. Powered by a Jetson Orin Nano 8GB processor with 256GB SSD, it delivers strong computing performance for AI-driven tasks. With 360° LiDAR, depth camera, and bumper sensors, Ariyan ensures safe and accurate navigation. Compact yet powerful, it supports a 10kg payload and runs on ROS 2 with up to 4 hours of battery life.

ROMOS with HAT

ROMOS with HAT is a developer-friendly robotics module powered by the STM32F407VET6 microcontroller, designed for both beginners and enthusiasts. Its compact, industrial-grade build reduces wiring complexity while supporting custom interface modules or HATs for versatile robotics applications. The included L298N-based motor driver HAT allows seamless control of up to four DC motors with a 2A current rating each. This powerful and expandable platform makes learning, building, and experimenting with robotics easy and efficient.

Tulip

Tulip is a compact educational robot designed to make robotics and coding accessible to students of all ages. With a user-friendly design, powerful computing capabilities, and multiple onboard sensors, Tulip enables hands-on, experiential learning in classrooms and at home. It supports advanced robotics applications like obstacle avoidance, line following, and basic mapping, making learning interactive and fun. Small in size but big on performance, Tulip is the perfect tool to spark creativity and innovation in young minds.

UBOT

UBOT is an advanced telepresence companion developed by Crobot Technologies, designed to enable real-time remote interaction and monitoring. Powered by NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano, it delivers high AI performance for seamless communication and navigation. Equipped with 360° LiDAR, depth cameras, touch display, and bumper sensors, UBOT ensures safe and intelligent mobility. Ideal for industrial monitoring, security, healthcare, education, and customer service, UBOT bridges the gap between virtual presence and real-world engagement.
