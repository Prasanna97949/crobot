Tulip

Tulip is a compact educational robot designed to ignite passion for robotics and coding among students of all ages. With its user-friendly design, multiple sensors, and powerful computing capabilities, Tulip makes experiential learning engaging, hands-on, and accessible for schools, colleges, and homes.

Key Highlights

Experiential Learning

Tulip offers interactive, hands-on robotics and coding lessons for students from beginner to advanced levels.

Multiple Sensors

Equipped with ultrasonic, infrared, IMU sensors, and optional LiDAR for advanced robotic functionalities.

Powerful System

Runs on Raspberry Pi 4 or NVIDIA Jetson with ROS 2 support for real-world robotics applications.

Educational Focus

Designed to inspire creativity and innovation, making robotics education accessible to schools and homes alike.

Programming Capabilities

Supports obstacle avoidance, maze solving, line following, terrain detection, and remote operation experiments.
